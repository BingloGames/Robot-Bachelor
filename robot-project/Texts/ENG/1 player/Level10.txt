A variable can store strings, strings are texts or words. Sometimes, you will want a partial part of a string. For this, we can slice the string from index a to index b like this:
	string[a:b]
This will give you all the letters in indexes a to b, b not included. 
To get a slice from the beginning to a certain index you can do: string[:b]
To get a slice from a certain index to the end you can do: string[a:]
